A single headline metric in a glass tile — community and course numbers.

```jsx
<Stat value="2,271" label="Thành viên" sublabel="đang hoạt động" />
<Stat value="33" label="Bài học" tone="dark" align="center" />
```

`value` is the big number, `label`/`sublabel` describe it. `tone="dark"` for navy sections, `align="center"` to center, `icon` for a leading glyph.
