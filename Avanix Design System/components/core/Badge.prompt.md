Small status or category label (e.g. "COURSE", "Beginner", "Learning").

```jsx
<Badge color="blue">Beginner</Badge>
<Badge color="success" dot>Learning</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid" color="navy">PRO</Badge>
```

`color`: blue | navy | success | warning | danger | neutral. `variant`: soft (default tint) | solid | glass. Add `dot` for a leading status indicator.
